Lasagna Recipe

Description

A popular recipe submitted over 20 years ago that has quickly become one of most popular recipes on Allrecipes. Follow along to see why this has become a favorite!

    Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
    Steps
  1. Make the meat sauce, cook the nodles and make the ricotta mixture
  2. Layer them following the order:meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella slices, meat sauce, parmesan cheese.
  3. Repeat until finished and then top with the remaining Parmesan.
  4. Put in an oven preheated to 375 degrees for 50 minutes. Cover with foil for the first 25 and cook uncovered without.
  5. Let it rest at room temperature for 15 minutes before cutting into it!
