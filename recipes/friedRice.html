Fried Rice Recipe

Description

A quick recipe that requires little preparation, what else could one ask for?? This top-rated recipe makes it easy to recreate a takeout favorite in under half an hour!

    Ingredients
  • 3/4 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 garlic clove (minced or to taste)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chillded white rice
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce (add more for taste preference)
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
    Steps
  1. Assemble and prepare ingredients
  2. Put carrots in small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to low boil and cook for 3-5 minutes. Stir in peas and drain.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat and pour in order vegetables, carrots/peas/garlic for about 30s. Add in eggs and stir quickly to scramble with vegetables
  4. Add cooked rice and soy sauce. Toss to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
